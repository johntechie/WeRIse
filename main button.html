Why Should you fundraise with HarambeeApp?





Your money is Guranteed.
Every time a donor contributes to your Harambee, the money will go straight into your mobile money account.
No Goal Requarements.
Keep the money you raise whether you beat your goal or not. No deadline.


Monitor your funds.
Keep tabs with your money as they trickle in. Both you and your donors will be able to view the amount of funds raised.
Anyone can Donate.
Whether young or old, you only need an mpesa account to start making donation's or setup an Harambee...make donation for as low as ksh 10.


Get 100% of funds Raised.
HarambeeApp is a charity website, Whatever your fortunate to raise will go directly to your account. 'WE DONT CHARGE', its free.
Raise 10x more & faster.
After launching an harambee, share it out with your friend on any social media platform, then your friends will share with there other friends. As a result it will quickly lead to faster fundraise.
